Various Fun Activities During The Trip

1. Paragliding Paragliding is not for the faint of heart.
But when you’re soaring over the seaside or swooping
through the mountains, any sense of fear is quickly
replaced by one of awe. In fact, paragliding is
probably the most awesome of all aerial pursuits.More Info
2. Scuba Diving Scuba diving is perhaps the best way to explore
the fantastic underwater world, getting up close with
the wonders and the secrets of this marine universe,
with its breathtakingly colourful and
diverse inhabitants.More Info
3. Camping There are few more rewarding feelings than pitching
your tent and spending the night beneath the stars.
Whether you want to escape to a remote mountainside
or find an idyllic coastal campsite,there are some
spectacular locations to discover.More Info
4. Skiing The majority of people who ski every year
are recreational skiers. If you like to get out,
ride the lifts and make a few turns at the local resort
or on vacation a few times a year, you are a
recreational skier. More Info
6. Climbing Some climbers prefer sport climbing on perfect tufas
with breathtaking sea views. Others will swear that
perfectly smooth granite walls in picturesque
forest landscapes are tops. More Info
7. Bungee Jumping Millions have taken the plunge,
with millions more right behind them to try
this adrenaline fueled activity.More Info
8. Hot Air Balloon Ride There’s no other experience like a
hot air balloon ride,Exploring a new
place from the basket of a hot air balloon
offers a perspective you can’t get any other way.More Info
9. Hiking One of the best ways to explore a country
is on foot, so strap on your shoe-barus!
Whether it is conquering a mountain,
passing through remote villages, or getting deeper
into jungle, hiking is guaranteed to make you feel
like a bonafide explorer. More Info
10. Snow Fun With majestic peaks, mouth-watering cuisine and
world-class skiing, the following winter havens
provide exceptional settings to enjoy the snow,
slopes and warmth of a fire. More Info
